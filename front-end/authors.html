About The Authors

   

Joe Parsley

The product of two loving, arabian unicorns, Joe is a fantabulous individual with just an overwhelming passion for Nicki Minaj. If you don't find him on the praerie prancing around with the other Unicorns in the neighborhood, you'll find him at home bobbing his head to the latest Minaj-erie.

   

Jared Luedtke

Jared is one of 18,000 deer that were cloned in a lab by cutting edge scientist based in Iceland. While deer aren't normally wandering around in their country, the scientist thought it would be "a nice change from pace out in the country side". The scientists soon realized that it was a terrible idea releasing them into the wild after about 99% of the deer population fell off due to traffic incidents. Long story short, Jared, is the sole remaining Icelandic deer.

   

Charles Emrich

Born as a boy even though his parents wanted a robot, Unit 24-B-9 (Charles, for short), has founded his whole life based upon one single, ultimate, powerful, obscene, goal; to become the greatest surfer the world has ever known. Together with his colony of penguins and the aid of the Beach Boys, Charles... Let's be honest, he's never going to be a good surfer.

   

Jared Eiseman

The unholy union of a meteorite and a sea witch birthed an abomination heretofor unknown. When he finally shambled ashore, after a difficult childhood spent hunting small fish, Jared discovered an aptitude for machinery that some would consider... unnatural. He has promised to help the world with his gifts, but the persistent smell of fish has kept the world from wanting to help him.
